The Perfectionist

Layershift's next generation Jelastic PaaS provides easy deployment of Java (Tomcat, Jetty), JEE (GlassFish, TomEE), PHP (Apache, Nginx), and Ruby (Apache + Passenger).

Combine these best in class application servers with your SQL (MySQL, MariaDB, PostgreSQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB, CouchDB) database server of choice to power your hack.

Pull your source code directly from your Github repos, and you can even build your Java project in the cloud via Maven.

(See platform documentation at http://docs.jelastic.com)

Each Great Uni Hackathon participant is granted a free £10 platform credit to use during the hackathon (and beyond).

Simply develop and launch your hackathon entry on our platform, and we'll give you an extra £25 free credit for each bug report or feature request that you submit.

The individual who submits the most reports/requests will win "The Perfectionist" prize: £500 extra free Jelastic credit (in addition to the £25 per report). It could be enough to host your awesome projects completely free for over a year!

Rules:

  • Reports must include your Jelastic account username and be submitted via our official report form
  • Each bug report must include steps to reproduce (we welcome and will still try to investigate non reproducible bugs, but if we can't validate the bug we can't give free credit make sure that your bug reports are clear!)
  • Feature requests must include descriptive use case(s)
  • Duplicate or incomplete submissions will not be counted
  • If multiple people report the same bug/feature, the earliest submission wins
  • Multiple (different) submissions per person are permitted (e.g. submit 10 bug reports, receive £250 free credit!)
